DIY DIY Oil Cleanser: Ingredients for 30ml Normal Skin ● ● Castor Oil - 15ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil- 15ml Normal Skin ● ● Castor Oil - 20ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil- 10ml Normal Skin ● ● Castor Oil - 20ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil- 10ml Preparation Mix all the ingredients in a 30ml container and store it in a dry place. To use, massage the oil and wipe it off with muslin cloth. Tips Castor oil has antioxidants which fights your skin from acne and has moisturising properties Olive oil is rich in vitamins. Massaging your skin with olive oil causes breakouts. Mix to the ratio to your skin types, massage your face and wipe it off with cloth or tissue.​Can add Vitamin E 5 drops to the oils. It is a powerful antioxidant.
