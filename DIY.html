HAND SANITIZER


Preparation Time: 5mts
Quantity : 100ml

INGREDIENTS:

Distilled Water - 30ml
Glycerine - 5ml
Isopropyl / Rubbing Alcohol - 60ml
Tea tree oil - 2ml
Eucalyptus Oil - 3ml

PREPARATION

Mix all the ingredients one by one in a spray bottle.
Seal the cap and shake well. Now sanitizer is ready to use

It is recommended to use 60% of alcohol in all sanitizer as per the centre of disease control

DIRECTIONS TO USE: Spray Sanitizer on your hands and rub well till it gets absorbed
